# Reply inbox before digest

ReviewInbox prioritizes the reply workflow before product digests. Replying to App Store and Google Play reviews is the immediate personal use case and creates the normalized review data needed for later weekly pain digests.

## Consequences

The V1 roadmap starts with multi-app ingestion, AI reply drafts, human approval, and store publishing. Weekly digests are part of the product direction but come after the reply inbox is useful end to end.
